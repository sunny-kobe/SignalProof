# 研究

## 要回答的问题

- 这个海外 AI 信号是否值得中文内容用户关注？
- 它能不能转成有来源、有反方、有行动清单的内容样例？
- 它是否有可复用资产候选？

## 已确认事实

- [Digiday creator authenticity and messiness demand](https://digiday.com/media/after-an-oversaturation-of-ai-generated-content-creators-authenticity-and-messiness-are-in-high-demand)：Reports 2026 demand for authenticity after AI-generated content oversaturation and cites declining preference for generative AI creator content.
- [O Dwyer PR News on trust and AI slop](https://www.odwyerpr.com/story/public/24534/2026-03-27/lack-trust-ai-slop-could-slow-social-media-growth.html)：Sprout Social survey coverage: AI-generated content without labels is a top thing respondents want brands to stop; many report seeing AI slop often.
- [Klaviyo consumer trust in AI](https://www.klaviyo.com/solutions/ai/consumer-trust-in-ai)：Consumer trust research: low-quality or generic AI content can reduce trust, especially among AI skeptics and holdouts.
- [University of Florida AI slop study coverage](https://news.ufl.edu/2026/03/ai-slop)：Frames AI slop as hurting consumers and creators, but high-quality AI can help professionals enhance work.
