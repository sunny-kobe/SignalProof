# 研究

## 要回答的问题

- 这个海外 AI 信号是否值得中文内容用户关注？
- 它能不能转成有来源、有反方、有行动清单的内容样例？
- 它是否有可复用资产候选？

## 已确认事实

- [Corporate Ink GEO and AI Visibility 2026 Report](https://corporateink.com/geo-and-ai-visibiliy-2026-report)：B2B tech marketers survey: 88% of CMOs/VP marketers are asked by leadership/board about AI visibility, but only 34% have defined AI visibility strategy.
- [Lumar 4-Pillar GEO Strategy Framework](https://www.lumar.io/blog/best-practice/4-pillar-geo-strategy-framework-for-ai-search-visibility)：GEO/AEO is framed as technical, content, entity and brand authority work; useful for turning abstract visibility into checklist.
- [Northwoods AI Search 2026 tool overview](https://www.nwsdigital.com/Blog/How-to-Optimize-Content-for-AI-Search-in-2026)：Shows market confusion and tool packaging around Semrush AI Toolkit, HubSpot AI Search Grader, Writesonic Analytics and AI SERP tools.

## 反证和替代方案

- GEO 供应商内容很多，容易变成工具营销二手整理。
- 国内目标用户可能还没被 AI search 直接压到业务指标，付费意图不确定。
